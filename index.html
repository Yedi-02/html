Academe Portal

The Academe Portal is designed for Filipino Educational institutions to streamline administrative tasks and enhance the learning experience for the students. With the Academe Portal, you can easily manage your school in a centralized platform from admission to your digital classroom.

Book a Demo NOW!

Your Easiest School Management System

The Academe Portal is specifically designed for Filipino educational institutions, taking into account the unique requirements and challenges faced by schools and universities in the Philippines.

With our platform, we aim to make school management easier, more efficient, and more effective for Filipino educators, administrators, and parents. Our system offers a wide range of features that cater to the various needs of educational institutions, such as admission management, student records management, attendance tracking, grading, and communication.

Your Partner in Digitalization

With the whole world slowly making the complete shift to digital landscapes, EPI Academe Portal’s main goal has been to make the transition as seamless as possible for schools, students, and parents.
